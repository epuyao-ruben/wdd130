Last Projects

Medlog Terminal

The Medlog Port Terminal Project consists of the creation of a 21[Ha] surface platform which serves as storage for containers coming from the Valparaíso port terminal. The development of the project included the analysis of survey points, maps and topographic data, the creation of a 3D model that represented the terrain and the large projected platform that meets the requirements of the client regarding the maximum allowed slopes, the calculation of the volume of earthworks to obtain a balance between the volume of cut and fill. It also included the hydrological study and hydraulic design of the interior drainage networks and a study of the flood risks resulting from massive landfilling. The volume of earthwork for this project is approximately 2,000,000[m3]

Los Laureles Project

The Medlog Port Terminal Project consists of the creation of a 21[Ha] surface platform which serves as storage for containers coming from the Valparaíso port terminal. The Los Laureles Project consists of a housing project that will allow more than 1350 low-income families to have a home which, through urbanization of land in the upper part of Valparaiso, create new roads and expand the city. The engineering development consists of the elaboration of the topography based on Lidar points, hydrological study, design of hydraulic networks and culverts, design of rigid pavements according to AASHTO methodology, preparation of land grading for the location of buildings in consolidated land area, design of interior roads and parking areas, and flood risk analysis for the channel that has a flow of 3[m3/ s]. The volume of earthwork for this project is approximately 2,000,000[m3]

Valle Pioneros Project

The Valle Pioneros project consists of the construction of 10 residential buildings that will serve as homes for people who lost their homes in the great fire in Valparaíso. The development included analysis of the topography and creation of a three-dimensional mantle for the subsequent creation of grading, estimation of earthworks, hydrological and hydraulic design, design of civil works such as pavements and pedestrian areas, technical reports, technical specifications, and a flood risk analysis project of the existing riverbed.
